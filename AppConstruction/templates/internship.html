Internship For Civil Engineering Candidates

SQ Engineering Contractors Pvt Ltd. offers, Internship for civil engineering students . Internship provides you an in-depth knowledge on civil department students. This internship enables the students to understand and learn the current trend in the job market. Internship provides great opportunity to get real life experience and exposure. Students will  prefer internships to  build their profile for their jobs and also for their higher studies. Based on performance, our company provides best offers for interns.

Learn More
Training Syllabus
  • Onsite Training & Site Execution – Road Works, Retaining & River Protection Wall, Underwater Construction, Heritage Works, Interior Designing & Commissioning, Onsite Safety Measures & Precautions, Equipments Needed and Waterbodies & Geotextile Works
  • Office Procedures & Documentation – Estimation & Billing, Central & State Govt BOQ Preparation, Online Tendering Process, Quantity Surveying & Material Calculations, Labours & Subcontractors Controlling and Necessary Accounting & GST Bill Preparation
  • Basic Tools in Auto CAD
  • KMBR & KPBR Basics
  • Introduction to Construction Materials & Chemicals – Construction Material Testing

Benefits of Doing a Civil Internships

Student can get a real world experience and also our company provides a hands on training in a professional environment. Internships help to getting a chance to try all the possible jobs with explore different options in career i.e, our company provides best offers for interns.

  • A chance to develop your knowledge and skills in a particular field or industry.
  • Exploring different roles to see which one you would like to pursue.
  • Gaining valuable work experience to set you apart from other candidates.

  • Great opportunity to create a network of contacts.
  • Applying the concepts and strategies of academic study in a live work environment.
